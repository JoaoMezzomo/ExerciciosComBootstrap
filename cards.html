Título do Cartão

Subtitulo do cartão

A guitar, which is mostly shown as an electric guitar and usually in generally red. Discontinued emoji sets or such as LG and HTC displayed this as an acoustic guitar, as did previous version of Windows and Twemoji. Guitar was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
